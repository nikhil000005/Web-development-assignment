About Proeat

Our Proeat Noodles and Dumplings is one of the
Asian - Chinese restaurant in Pakenham, VIC which is well
known for the specialized dishes such as Proeat
Entrees, Proeat Malaysian Chinese, Proeat Dumplings (Steam or
Fried), Proeat Noodles, Proeat Soup Noodles, Proeat
Rice Dish, Extra dipping sauce and a lot more. We
serve and present our delicious food with great
pleasure and practice to make your meal and time
prominently enjoyable. Our services also include
takeaway with the comfort of ordering online
through ozfoodhunter.
